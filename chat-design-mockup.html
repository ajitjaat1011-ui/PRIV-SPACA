What changed vs. Instagram's layout:
• Groups tab is first — your Community Channels (general-group, announcements, etc.), with a Pinned section on top.
• Primary tab is second — 1-to-1 DMs, newest first, with unread badges and rich previews (photo, voice, 🔒 encrypted, story reactions).
• Requests is no longer a separate tab — it's a tappable banner at the top of Primary (opens a filtered list of people you don't follow).
• Matches your real theme: dark #0b1620 background, #00a2ff accent, online dots, same avatars.
