        Terms & Community Guidelines

        Version 1.0 · Effective 30 Jun 2026

        1. Private community. PRIV SPACA is a small invite-style space. Be kind, be real, and treat every member with respect.
        2. No harassment, hate, or doxxing. Personal attacks, slurs, threats, or sharing someone's private info will result in account removal.
        3. No illegal content. Don't share or solicit anything illegal — drugs, weapons, CSAM, malware, stolen accounts, etc.
        4. You own what you post. Don't upload media you don't have the right to share. You grant PRIV SPACA permission to display your content to other members of this community.
        5. One person, real accounts. No bots, no scraping, no automated mass-DMs. Don't impersonate another person.
        6. Security & data. Your password is hashed (bcrypt). Your 4-digit PIN is hashed too and is the only password-recovery key. Don't share your PIN. We never ask for it via DM.
        7. Image uploads. Profile photos & post images are stored on our GitHub-backed CDN. Posts/messages are stored in plain-text JSON for the community to read. Don't share secrets in chat.
        8. Free service, no warranty. PRIV SPACA is provided as-is. We may go offline, reset data, or change features without notice.
        9. Account actions. Use the Block button to silence anyone who's bothering you. Reports go to the community admin (Arvindjaat).
        10. Age. You must be at least 13 years old to use PRIV SPACA.

        By creating an account, you confirm you have read and agree to these terms. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account — just ask the admin.
